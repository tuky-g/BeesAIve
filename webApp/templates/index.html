About BeesAIve

BeesAIve Project comes for love for nature and wants to focus in the importance of bees in the environment and in the human survival.

Using machine and deep learning techniques, after pre-processing more the 12000 images of bees, wasp, other insects and other things, we have been able to predict what a given picture is with more than the 90% of accuracy

The final goal of this project is to create an alert system that warns the beekeeper of foreign species approaching the honeycomb

BeesAIve has been developing in the context of Saturdays AI program whose objective is to spread and teach artificial intelligence in all fields.
